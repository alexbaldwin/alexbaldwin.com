Merry Christmas Stephanie, you gorgeous nerd

❅ ❅ ❅

You are wonderful. I tried making this card with crayons and printer paper but it's just not my best medium, you deserved better. So I decided to cheat and surprise, use the medium I am best at.

This last year has been so much fun. Since we met, I immediately knew you and I were going to become close. The night of our awkward dinner before The Revenant, you had me cracking up guessing everyone's middle names. Didn't take you very long to get "Scott" out of me but the hints did help. Here's what I wrote:

“Met Stephanie Marie last night, she's good at guessing middle names.”

— January 16th, 2016

Although there's not many photos from the first few months of us dating, I'm glad we've started to fix that. You even got me to take some selfies which is unprescedented. Damn, we look good together. It's amazing that all the magical photos in Harry Potter aren't selfies. Mugshots don't count.

When you came home with a Christmas tree, I was initially slightly skeptical. I don't even decorate normally, none the less for holidays. However, walking home from work to the smell of a fresh tree and Christmas music playing has been fantastic. Your joke about the minimal tree really stuck with me and I chuckle every time I think about it now.

It's a small thing that makes me so happy. We can come up with our own traditions that complement both of us so well. Thanks for bringing it home, totally worth the struggles and soreness.

❅ ❅ ❅

Looking back on our past year of photos, I can't believe how much great food we've made together. You really egged me on to cook bigger and better things this year. We make magic happen in the kitchen.

❅ ❅ ❅

You're the best and I can't imagine a life without you. Traveling all over, accidentally matching, and cracking dad jokes with you can't be beat. I'm so glad I grilled you about sci-fi and that you got bored watching Seven Samurai. While I was abroad in the spring, despite us spending relatively little time together, I thought about you all the time. Our Flora Grubb date while being jet lagged was so nice and I was so excited to see you.

Being together this past year has been the greatest gift and I'm so excited to see what unfolds next.

Love,
Alexander Scott
